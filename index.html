KLS ERP: Redefining B2B Chemical ERP

Slide 1: The Vision

KLS

A high-performance catalogue, compliance engine, and quotation platform tailored for chemical distributors.

  • The Industry Challenge: Chemical distribution is bogged down by manual document preparation, complex regulatory requirements (SDS/COA), and delayed quotation turnarounds.
  • The Solution: An integrated ERP that combines a modern storefront with automated regulatory PDF rendering, AI demand insights, and real-time messaging pipelines.
Design Philosophy: Engineered with a clean, high-contrast visual system using custom brand tokens (Ink steel-blue and burnt-orange ember highlights).

Automating Compliance & Documents

Slide 2: The Core Game-Changers

KLS
  • Excel Upload to COA PDF: Parses raw spreadsheets and automatically formats elegant Certificate of Analysis PDFs in seconds.
  • Dynamic SDS Compliance: Automatically syncs hazard statements, transport codes, and storage instructions in compliant format.
  • Instant Verification: Immediate interactive PDF rendering directly within the browser application.
Zero Copy-Paste: The manual extraction of specs from lab notebooks is replaced with a single drag-and-drop.

Gmail & WhatsApp Integrations

Slide 3: Omni-Channel Notifications

KLS

Closing sales faster by delivering instant notifications directly where customers are active.

  • Automated Gmail Dispatch: Customers receive a beautifully branded HTML email quotation summary immediately after cart submission, complete with direct click-to-call sales buttons.
  • WhatsApp Instant Messaging: Real-time quotation notifications sent automatically to customer mobile devices using secure Twilio APIs.

Tailored Stakeholder Experiences

Slide 4: Effortless Workflows

KLS
  • For B2B Customers: Find chemicals instantly using advanced filters and molecular formula cards. Request quotes in 3 clicks.

  • For Administrators: Review submitted catalog items, SDS sheets, and COAs via a side-by-side Diff Viewer to ensure quality control.
